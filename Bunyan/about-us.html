What is real estate investment?

Real estate investment involves purchasing, owning, managing, and selling properties for profit. This can include residential, commercial, or industrial properties. Investors aim to generate rental income or capital appreciation over time.

How can I get started in property investment?

To get started, first, research the market and understand the types of properties that suit your investment goals. You can either buy properties directly or invest through real estate funds or platforms that offer investment opportunities in real estate.

What are the risks of investing in real estate?

The risks of real estate investment include market fluctuations, property maintenance costs, vacancies, and the potential for depreciation. It's essential to conduct thorough research and diversify investments to mitigate risks.
